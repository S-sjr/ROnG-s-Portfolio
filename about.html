ROnG

Jiarong Sun (b.2003, Shanxi, China) is a multimedia art designer with a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media Arts from Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University. Sun's creative practice spans graphic design, 3D modeling & animation, video post-production, and interactive media. He specializes in merging artistic storytelling with cutting-edge tools such as Unreal Engine 5, TouchDesigner, and the Adobe Creative Suite to create immersive digital experiences.

Throughout the academic journey, Sun has led and contributed to various interdisciplinary projects, including serving as a student representative and designer for the MEGA International Creative Media Festival. Sun also actively participated in organizing university-wide career fairs and design exhibitions as the Deputy Director of the SCDA publicity department.

With a strong foundation in both design and technology, Sun sees multimedia as a powerful medium that connects people, ideas, and digital systems. As he embarks on further postgraduate studies in Animation and Visual Effects in Australia, the goal is to push creative boundaries and continue exploring innovative visual narratives.
